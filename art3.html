Poster

For this project, I created a hypothetical Poser by compositing images together within Photoshop. I then added additional text by using Illustrator. I came up with an idea of a dwarf made help advertisement. These dwarves need more guards to guard their dwarven treasure, so they made a poster! I enjoy whimsical and fantasy themes as shown in this piece.
