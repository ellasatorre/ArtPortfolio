Pixel Art GIF

In order to create this GIF, I first created a character I wanted to animate and a background. I choose to create this bunny character, who ends up appearing in many of my other pieces. I created a simple background reminiscent of a nicer indoor pool. I then worked on animating both the water and the bunny using Photoshop. I wanted to create a loop effect and did so by making the starting and ending frames the same.
